03 — APPROACH

Credit analysis taught me to distrust a number until I've traced where it came from.

Before financial modeling, I spent five years underwriting commercial credit at ICICI Bank and Axis Bank — reading financials for what they don't say, not just what they do. That instinct now shapes how I build models: every assumption has to trace back to a source, every scenario has to survive a "why."

An MS in Financial Analytics at Santa Clara University and a market-risk practicum at First Citizens Bank — using Bloomberg Terminal and SEC filings to analyze exposure across public companies — sharpened that into tools FP&A teams actually use: driver-based models, scenario tools, and memos that get read.
